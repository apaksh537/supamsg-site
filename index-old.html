Got questions?

How does SupaMsg work?
+
SupaMsg loads WhatsApp Web in isolated sessions inside a desktop app. Each account runs independently — just like using multiple Chrome tabs, but with AI, automations, and 48 other features built in.
Is it safe? Will my account get banned?
+
SupaMsg uses the official WhatsApp Web interface — the same as using it in your browser. No unofficial APIs, no account modifications. Your account stays safe.
Do you read my messages?
+
No. Absolutely not. We have zero access to your messages. Everything is end-to-end encrypted as per WhatsApp's security. All data stays on your device — no cloud, no servers, nothing.
Does it work when my phone is off?
+
Yes! WhatsApp's linked devices feature works independently, so you can use SupaMsg even when your phone is off or disconnected.
Can I try before paying?
+
SupaMsg is free forever for up to 3 WhatsApp accounts. No credit card needed. When you're ready for more, all paid plans come with a 30-day money-back guarantee.
