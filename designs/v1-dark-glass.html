SupaMsg runs as a desktop application on your Mac or Windows computer. It uses the official WhatsApp Web protocol to connect your accounts. Absolutely. SupaMsg uses the same WhatsApp Web protocol that powers the official WhatsApp desktop app. Your messages remain end-to-end encrypted. We don't have any servers that store or relay your messages — everything runs locally on your device.
